Description:

A classic - delicious, protein packed pizza.

Ingredients:

  • Flour
  • Vital Wheat Gluten
  • Active Dry Yeast
  • Chicken Breast
  • Garlic
  • Fat Free Mozzarella
  • Turkey Pepperoni
  • Alfredo Sauce

Steps

  1. Mix 110 grams of Flour, 20 grams of Vital Wheat Gluten, and 1.5 grams of Active Dry Yeast in a food processor.
  2. Add ~30 grams of water to the blender at a time, stop at 90 grams of water.
  3. Get dough out of processor, roll into a ball, put in a bowl and cover with cling wrap. Stick it in the fridge for 3+ hours.
  4. Take dough out of fridge, let sit for 30 minutes to an hour, then form it into a pizza shape.
  5. Add Alfredo Sauce first, then mozzarella, then chopped garlic, pepperoni, and chicken breast
  6. Cook at 400 degrees F for 25 minutes
  7. Enjoy!
