Description

A burger that's so good some say it's like crack

Igredneints:

  • Buns
  • 96% Lean Meat
  • Ranch Seasoning
  • Light Sour Cream

Steps

  1. Mix Seasoning, 135 grams Light Sour Cream, 454 grams 96% Lean Meat together
  2. Form into 4 patties
  3. Cook in air fryer at 400 for 15 minutes, flipping halfway
  4. Put on buns with whatever toppings and enjoy!
